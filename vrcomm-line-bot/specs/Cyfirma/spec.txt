CYFIRMA — Product Spec Sheet
Website: https://www.cyfirma.com/
Category: Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI), External Attack Surface Management (EASM), Dark Web Monitoring

PRODUCT OVERVIEW
================
CYFIRMA is an External Threat Landscape Management (ETLM) platform that provides
continuous threat intelligence, dark web monitoring, and external attack surface visibility.
It gives organisations advance warning of targeted attacks before they happen.

PRODUCT PLATFORM: DeCYFIR
==========================

1. Threat Intelligence (CTI)
   - Real-time intelligence from 200,000+ dark web, deep web, and open-source sources
   - Nation-state actor tracking: APT groups, ransomware gangs targeting your sector
   - Industry-specific threat feeds: financial, healthcare, government, manufacturing
   - Indicators of Compromise (IoC): IPs, domains, hashes, URLs — ready to import to SIEM/firewall
   - Threat actor profiles: motivation, TTPs (MITRE ATT&CK mapped), tools used
   - Early warning: detect when your brand/IP/domain is mentioned in attacker forums

2. External Attack Surface Management (EASM)
   - Continuously discover your internet-facing assets: IPs, domains, subdomains, cloud resources
   - Identify exposed services: open ports, misconfigured services, unpatched software
   - Shadow IT detection: find cloud assets your IT team doesn't know about
   - Certificate monitoring: expired/weak certificates on public-facing services
   - Vulnerability correlation: map discovered assets to CVEs and active exploits

3. Brand Intelligence & Phishing Detection
   - Monitor for fake domains impersonating your brand (typosquatting)
   - Detect phishing pages and fraudulent mobile apps using your brand
   - Social media monitoring: impersonation accounts on Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter
   - Takedown support: evidence package for domain/page takedown requests

4. Dark Web Monitoring
   - Monitor dark web forums and marketplaces for:
     - Leaked credentials (email/password combos from your domain)
     - Stolen data for sale (databases, customer records)
     - Discussions about attacking your organisation
     - Ransomware group communications targeting your sector
   - Alerts: immediate notification when your data appears in dark web

5. Vulnerability Intelligence
   - Correlate your asset inventory with CVE database
   - Prioritise patching by exploit availability and active exploitation in the wild
   - Zero-day early warning: detect vendor advisories before public disclosure
   - Patch management guidance: recommended remediation timeline per CVE

KEY DIFFERENTIATORS
====================
- Predictive intelligence: warning 3-6 weeks before attack, not after
- Thai-language threat reports available for local context
- Southeast Asia regional threat coverage: APT41, Lazarus, local cybercrime groups
- MITRE ATT&CK framework alignment for all threat intelligence

DELIVERY MODEL
==============
- SaaS platform (DeCYFIR web console)
- No on-premise deployment required
- API: export IoCs and alerts to SIEM, SOAR, firewall
- Reports: executive, technical, and compliance formats
- Threat analyst support: CYFIRMA analysts available for threat briefings

LICENSING
=========
- Annual subscription per platform module
- Tiers: Starter (CTI only) → Professional (CTI + EASM) → Enterprise (full platform)
- Pricing based on organisation size (employee count / domain count)
- Add-ons: brand monitoring, supply chain intelligence, managed threat briefing

COMMON USE CASES AT VRCOMM CUSTOMERS
======================================
- Know before you're attacked: early warning from dark web intelligence
- Leaked credential detection: find out if employee credentials are for sale
- Phishing prevention: detect fake domains before customers are victimised
- Executive threat briefing: monthly report for CISO/board on threats facing the organisation
- PDPA compliance: know if customer data has been leaked and respond promptly

PAIRING WITH OTHER VRCOMM PRODUCTS
====================================
- Cyfirma + LogPoint: push CYFIRMA IoCs to LogPoint SIEM for automated blocking
- Cyfirma + Hillstone/Sangfor: import threat intel feeds to firewall block list
- Cyfirma + Sophos Intercept X: IoC-based endpoint blocking via Sophos Live Response
- Cyfirma + Trellix: enrich Trellix XDR with CYFIRMA external threat context
- Cyfirma + Ubika WAF: block malicious IPs on WAF based on CYFIRMA threat feeds
